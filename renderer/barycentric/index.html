Function: Barycentric

Computes the barycentric coordinates of a given point in a given triangle.

Inputs:

  • A triangle given by vertices: (x0, y0), (x1, y1), (x2, y2)
  • A point: (px, py)

Outputs:

Algorithm: 

                    
Let u = [x2 - x0, x1 - x0, x0 - px]
Let v = [y2 - y0, y1 - y0, y0 - py]
u x v
